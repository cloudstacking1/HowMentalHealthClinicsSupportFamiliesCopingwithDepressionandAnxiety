The Therapy Process: What You Can Expect

Initial Intake & Verification

When you search for a mental health clinic near me, or look for a mental health specialist, it’s helpful to have a decision-making checklist. Here’s what you should consider:

After the initial phase, we offer maintenance planning, referrals, and emergency protocols to ensure continued support beyond core treatment.
